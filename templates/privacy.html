Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

At the Student Organization Management System (SOMS), we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information within the Laguna State Polytechnic University (LSPU) CCS environment.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when you register for an account or update your profile. This includes:

  • Personal identification (Name, Student ID Number).
  • Academic details (Program, Year Level, Section, Major).
  • Digital assets (Profile pictures, Digital signatures).
  • Contact information (Email address).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for the purpose of managing student organization records and activities. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Verify your identity as a bona fide student of CCS.
  • Process organizational requirements and clearances.
  • Generate automated reports for the SBO officers.
  • Facilitate communication regarding organization announcements.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Your digital signatures and personal data are stored in a secure database restricted to authorized SBO officers and administrators only. We do not sell or share your data with third parties outside of the university context.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal data stored in our system. If you believe any information we hold about you is incorrect, you may update it directly through your profile settings or contact an administrator.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the CCS Student Body Organization or the system developers.
