About Us

Welcome to the Student Organization Management System (SOMS). This platform is designed to digitize and centralize the administrative tasks of student organizations within Laguna State Polytechnic University.

Our Mission

To provide an efficient, transparent, and user-friendly environment for student leaders and members. We aim to replace manual paperwork with automated processes for membership management, event tracking, and reporting.

The Developers

This system was developed by the students of BSIT 4C AMG B as part of our commitment to technological innovation in education.
