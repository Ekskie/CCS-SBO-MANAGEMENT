About Our Mission

We are digitizing the way Student Organizations operate at LSPU. By bridging the gap between students and administration, we create transparent, efficient, and accessible governance.

Why SOMS?

The College of Computer Studies (CCS) Student Body Organization (SBO) Management System was developed to address the logistical challenges of managing thousands of student records manually.

Our platform eliminates paper trails, speeds up the validation process for student requirements (like IDs and signatures), and provides real-time data to organization leaders.

Efficiency First

Automating repetitive tasks so officers can focus on leadership.

Secure Data

Your data is protected with modern encryption and strict access controls.
